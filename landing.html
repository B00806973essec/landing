What does The Veggie Table do different than others ?

This start-up is a comprehensive listing of all the food stalls in Singapore that offer vegetarian meals. It serves as a one-stop platform for vegetarians and vegans to discover and explore new dining options in the city-state. The start-up provides detailed information about each stall, including their menu, operating hours, location, and ratings/reviews from customers. It also allows users to search for stalls based on their dietary preferences and location, making it easy for them to find exactly what they're looking for. Overall, the start-up aims to promote vegetarianism and make it more accessible to people in Singapore.
